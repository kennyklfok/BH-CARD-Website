Holter & Event Monitors

Holter Monitors and Event Monitors are portable devices used to continuously record your heart rhythm.

Holter Monitors record for 24 hours.

Event Monitors record for up two weeks.

What to Expect

Holter Monitor:

  • Five stickers (electrodes) will be placed on your chest.
  • Please do not get the device wet.
    • No bathing, showering, or swimming is allowed.
  • Record any relevant symptoms in the diary provided.

Event Monitor:

  • Three stickers (electrodes) will be placed on your chest.
  • Do not bathe or shower while wearing the monitor.
    • You will be shown how to change the stickers so the monitor can be removed for bathing or showering.
  • Record any relevant symptoms in the diary provided.

Returning the Monitor

  • Once the recording duration is complete, follow the specific instructions to take off the monitor.
  • Return the monitor to the drop-off box by the agreed upon date/time discussed during your appointment.
