Pacemaker Clinic

The Pacemaker Clinic monitors and manages implanted cardiac devices such as pacemakers and implantable cardioverter defibrillators (ICDs).

What to Expect

  • Your device will be checked using specialized equipment to ensure it is functioning properly.
  • Battery status, heart rhythm data, and device settings will be reviewed.
  • Adjustments may be made if required to optimize device performance.
  • You will have the opportunity to schedule your next follow-up appointment before leaving the clinic.
